Who we are?

The precious you (TPY) is a non-profit organization which supports the equality and equity of the girl child and women irrespective of age, race, religion, origin and cultural norms.we act more as a civil society and information provision organisation, bringing women communities together for collective action, mobilizing them to articulate demands and voice concerns for other women. We help equip and empower females across distinctions of society to help curb the effects of classes amongst them. We seek to pursue a radical empowerment based approach through active outreach and life changing programmes. Our sole purpose is to act as an implementer and catalyst of change through our ability to inspire, facilitate improved thinking and action to promote social transfer.
